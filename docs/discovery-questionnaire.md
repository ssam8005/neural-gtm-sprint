# Neural-GTM Sprint — Pre-Sprint Discovery Questionnaire
*Complete before the 30-min discovery call. Takes 15–20 minutes.*

---

## Section 1: Company & Revenue Context

1. Current Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) or Gross Annual Revenue?
2. Primary business model? (B2B SaaS / E-commerce / Professional Services / Other)
3. How many people on your sales/revenue team (SDRs, AEs, RevOps, marketing)?
4. Current average monthly lead volume (new leads entering top of funnel)?
5. Current lead-to-opportunity conversion rate (approximate)?
6. Average deal size / average order value?

## Section 3: Pain Points

10. Where do leads most commonly fall through the cracks?
11. How many hours/week does your team spend on manual data entry, enrichment, or routing?
12. What percentage of your CRM contacts have complete, accurate data?
13. How stale is your existing CRM data? When was it last enriched?
14. Are there deals marked "closed lost" or "dead" that were never re-engaged?
15. What is the biggest bottleneck between lead entry and first rep contact?

---

## Section 4: AI & Automation Maturity

16. Have you deployed any AI or LLM tools in your revenue workflow?
17. Do you have any n8n, Zapier, or Make workflows in production?
18. Have you used Clay or similar enrichment platforms? What was the outcome?
19. What has been your biggest challenge with AI or automation tools?
20. Does your team have technical members who can maintain automation post-deployment?

---

## Section 5: Compliance & Data

21. Does your business operate in a regulated industry (Healthcare, Finance, Legal)?
22. Do you have data residency or sovereignty requirements?
23. What is your current approach to AI output governance?
24. Are there customer data categories that cannot be processed by third-party AI?

---

## Section 6: Sprint Goals

25. What does success look like in 30 days if the sprint works completely?
26. What is the single most important outcome you need?
27. What is your current timeline pressure?
28. Who is the internal champion, and who has budget authority?
29. Are there internal constraints we should know about before scoping?
30. What has prevented you from solving these problems internally until now?
